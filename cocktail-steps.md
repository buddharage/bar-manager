# Cocktail Steps - Ready to Paste into xtraCHEF

Copy the steps for each cocktail into the Instructions field under Procedures.

## A Shade of Jade ✅ (already added)
1. Shake all ingredients
2. Strain over ice into rocks glass
3. Garnish with Mint and Lemon wheel

## Amulet ✅ (already added)
1. Stir all ingredients
2. Strain into rocks glass
3. Garnish with dehydrated lime wheel
4. Spray entire drink with lime bitters spray

## Bishops Absolution
1. Stir all ingredients in mixing glass
2. Strain into Nick & Nora glass
3. Garnish with one cherry on a pick

## Blood Moon
1. Stir
2. Strain into flute glass
3. Top with seltzer
4. Garnish with rose petal

## Blood Shot
1. Shake
2. Strain into shot glass

## Calypsos Secret
1. Shake all ingredients (longer than usual)
2. Double strain into nick and nora glass
3. Smoke bubble on top

## Celosa Luna
1. Shake
2. Double strain into a rocks glass with a powdered cream king cube
3. Garnish with cinnamon sugar using moon stencil

## Crystal Lake
1. Shake batch and lime
2. Double Strain into Nick & Nora
3. Garnish with a lime wheel

## Devil Fruit Shot
1. Add pomegranate seed to shot glass
2. Pour

## Dia De Sandia
1. Salt half rim rocks glass
2. Shake
3. Strain over watermelon ice cube (50/50 syrup + water)
4. Garnish with lime wheel

## E.S.P. (Espresso Spirit Potion)
1. Shake ingredients to create a bit of foam
2. Strain into rocks glass with king cube
3. Garnish generously with nutmeg

## Empress
1. Shake all ingredients in ice
2. Strain into tin shaker
3. Reverse dry shake
4. Double strain over ice in collins glass
5. Top with soda
6. Garnish w/ cherry on a stick

## Falling Leaves
1. Pour batch into rocks glass with a big ice cube (no stirring)

## Glinda
1. Shake all ingredients
2. Strain into tin and dry shake hard
3. Double strain into coupe glass
4. Garnish with dried flower

## Golden Hour
1. Shake
2. Dry shake
3. Pour into rocks glass with big cube
4. Garnish with dehydrated lemon wheel and flower on top

## Holy Mole
1. Stir
2. Pour into Nick and Nora glass
3. Garnish with lime wedge

## Jungle Bird
1. Shake all ingredients
2. Strain into highball glass
3. Garnish with skewered pineapple pieces

## King Ink
1. Shake
2. Double strain into coupe
3. Garnish with orange peel and cherry on toothpick

## Lovespell
1. Shake, dry shake, double strain into coupe with dry basil leaf garnish

## Madonnas Moon
1. Crush ice
2. Shake all ingredients except ango
3. Strain over crushed ice into rocks glass
4. Dash ango bitters on top
5. Garnish with Lime Wheel

## Magic Mirror
1. Stir
2. Hawthorn strain into Nick and Nora
3. Express lemon peel (discard)
4. Garnish with skeleton leaf

## Mary Sol

2. Glass: wine glass
3. Garnish: Dehydrated Pineapple

## Midnight Silk
1. Stir
2. Strain into coupe

## Midnight Sun
1. Stir all ingredients in mixing glass

## Misty Morning
1. Muddle mint
2. Add ingredients
3. Roll shake
4. Dirty dump into Collins glass
5. Top with soda
6. Garnish with dehydrated lime wheel

## Mother Goddess
1. Muddle 8 coffee beans
2. Add all ingredients to tin and shake
3. Double strain over crushed ice into collins glass
4. Garnish with mint and orange wedge

## Nightingale
1. Shake all ingredients with ice

3. Double strain big rock in rocks glass
4. Garnish with rosemary sprig, or a white flower

## Peaceful Reverie

## Preacher's Daughter
